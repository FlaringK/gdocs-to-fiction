gdocs-to-fiction
by FlaringK

Welcome to "GDocs to Fiction", a page for formatting your google docs for fanfiction websites. To get started, head to the google doc you'd like to format and download it as a zipped Webpage (Go to File > Download > Web Page). Then drag and drop that zip file below to watch the magic happen

If you have any questions, trouble or feedback about this tool, don't hesitate to contact me on discord at FlaringK#4275

Homestuck formatting
(What is this?) This will add formatting to the original Gdoc to emulate a pesterlog from the webcomic homestuck.

Automatically it will detect all Homestuck chumhandles and the names of all homstuck characters who talk.

The alpha kids handle colours can be accessed by adding a "~" to the end of their handle. This is also how to switch between Calliope and Caliborn.

It will also detect notifications, so long as the line of text both begins and ends with "--".
(example) Before:

And so they started a converation

TT: Hello I am Rose

GG: hello i am jade

TT~: Hello I am Dirk

KARKAT: HELLO I AM KARKAT

AA: hell0 i am aradia 0u0

It was a very cool converstation

After:

Add custom characters
You can add custom characters here for the homestuck formatter. The formatter goes line by line, a character's name will be detected if the line starts with their name/handle followed by a colon (:). The name/handle must also be uppercase. The characters will be saved to your browser, so don't worry about having to redo them every visit.
Don't forget to save!
